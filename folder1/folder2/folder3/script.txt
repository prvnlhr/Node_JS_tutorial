ek bechain bhoot jo chaayaon aur roshni ke beech atka hua hai. 
Kya aap meri upasthiti mehsoos kar sakte hain? Ek thandi si hava 
jo aapki reedh ki haddi ko chu jaati hai, ek halki si aah jo hawa 
ko hilati hai? Meri kahani adhuri hai, mera astitva andhkaar mein 
ek chhanik saans ki tarah hai. Kabhi, main bhi jeeta tha, meri hansi 
in kamron mein goonjti thi, jo ab sirf khamoshi se bhare hue hain. 
Kya aap sun sakte hain mere ateet ki goonj, woh yaadein jo in deewaron 
se chipak gayi hain jaise ek purani kahani? Main aapko aapki aankhon 
ke kinare se dekhta hoon, ek parchai jo kabhi insaan thi, yaad kiya 
jane ki tamanna karta hoon. Dhyan se suno, kyunki meri awaaz mein woh 
sab hai jo bhool gaya hai, ek bhoot ka dard jo jeeviton ke dil mein shanti
